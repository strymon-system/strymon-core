Starts a new executor instance and blocks until termination.

Initiates the connection to the coordinator, sends an executor registration request and upon successful initialization, drives the ExecutorService with incoming requests from the coordinator.

The service is terminated either until SIGTERM is received or the connection to the coordinator is closed.
